## Western Aspect/Element Weights

Western scoring uses **dynamic weighting** based on Chinese pattern strength:

### Dynamic Weight System
- **Strong patterns** (San He, Liu He, Same Sign, Liu Chong, Liu Hai, Xing, Po): **70% Chinese / 30% Western**
- **Neutral patterns** (none, cross_trine, same_trine): **65% Chinese / 35% Western**

This allows Western aspects to have more influence when Chinese patterns are weak or neutral.

### Western Aspects
- Trine: +10
- Sextile: +6

- Opposition: +2
- Square: -6
- Quincunx: -4

### Western Elements
