Delete Your AstroMatch Account

Request account deletion and data removal

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Open the AstroMatch app on your device
  2. Go to Settings or Profile
  3. Select "Delete Account" or "Account Settings"
  4. Follow the prompts to confirm account deletion
  5. Alternatively, contact us using the information below

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your profile information (name, photos, bio, etc.)
  • Your account credentials and authentication data
  • Your match history and conversations
  • Your preferences and settings
  • Your app usage data

Data Retention

Some data may be retained for legal or operational purposes:

  • Transaction records (if applicable) - retained for 7 years for tax/legal compliance
  • Anonymized usage analytics - retained in aggregated form without personal identifiers

All personally identifiable information will be deleted within 30 days of your request.

⚠️ Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your account is deleted, you will lose access to all your matches, messages, and profile data.
