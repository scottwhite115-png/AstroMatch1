Child Safety Standards

AstroMatch's Commitment to Protecting Children from Sexual Abuse and Exploitation

Our Commitment

AstroMatch is committed to protecting children from sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We have zero tolerance for any content, behavior, or activity that endangers children or violates child safety laws.

Age Restrictions

  • Minimum Age Requirement: AstroMatch is restricted to users aged 18 and older.
  • Age Verification: We require users to provide their date of birth during account creation and verify they are 18 or older.
  • No Minors Allowed: We do not permit users under the age of 18 to create accounts or use our services under any circumstances.

Content Moderation

  • Automated Screening: We use automated systems to detect and prevent inappropriate content, including content that may involve minors.
  • Human Review: All reported content is reviewed by trained moderators.
  • Zero Tolerance: Any content, images, or communications involving minors in a sexual context are immediately removed and reported to authorities.
  • Profile Verification: We verify user profiles and may request additional identification to confirm age and identity.

Reporting Mechanisms

  • In-App Reporting: Users can report suspicious behavior, inappropriate content, or concerns about child safety directly through the app.
  • 24/7 Monitoring: Our moderation team monitors reports around the clock.
  • Law Enforcement Cooperation: We immediately report any suspected child exploitation to relevant law enforcement agencies.
  • NCMEC Reporting: We report suspected child sexual abuse material to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and similar organizations in applicable jurisdictions.

User Safety Features

  • Block and Report: Users can block and report other users who engage in inappropriate behavior.
  • Privacy Controls: Users have control over who can contact them and view their profiles.
  • Safety Tips: We provide safety guidelines and tips to help users stay safe while using our platform.
  • Account Suspension: Accounts that violate our safety policies are immediately suspended or permanently banned.

Staff Training

  • All moderation staff receive training on identifying and responding to child safety concerns.
  • We maintain protocols for escalating serious safety issues to law enforcement.
  • Regular training updates ensure our team stays current with best practices in child protection.

Legal Compliance

  • We comply with all applicable laws regarding child protection, including but not limited to laws in the United States, European Union, and other jurisdictions where we operate.
  • We cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations related to child safety.
  • We maintain records as required by law to assist in investigations.
⚠️ Important: If you encounter any content or behavior that you believe involves child exploitation, please report it immediately through our app or contact us directly. We take all reports seriously and act quickly to protect children.
